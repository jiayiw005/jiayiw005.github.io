Hello:)

Hi! I'm an undergraduate studying Mathematics-Computer Science (Sc.B.) and Applied Mathematics (A.B.) at Brown University.

I hope to build the formal and empirical infrastructure for trustworthy machine learning across two complementary dimensions:

  • On the construction side, model architectures informed by various learning paradigms1 (neurosymbolic programming2 in particular), and how they enable more robust and interpretable reasoning models.
  • On the guarantee side, evaluation and auditing pipeline design3 informed by formal verification5, 1, and how they scaffold more aligned and accountable automated systems.

Concretely, I've been interested in formal theorem proving and program synthesis as fully specified, unambiguous, machine-checkable substrates for studying models' reasoning processes; I'm also interested in how they interact and integrate with general-purpose foundation-model systems.
