### About Me

Hi! I'm an undergraduate studying Mathematics-Computer Science and Applied Mathematics at Brown University. 

I aim to build the *formal* and *empirical* infrastructure for **trustworthy and accountable machine learning** across two complementary dimensions: 
1. on the *learning* side, structured representations and neurosymbolic architectures, and how they support more robust and interpretable model reasoning processes<sup>[1](/projects/#lean4-proof-mining), [2](/projects/#mot)</sup>
2. on the *guarantee* side, evaluation<sup>[3](/projects/#overton-evals)</sup> and auditing pipeline design<sup>[4](/projects/#am-auditing)</sup> informed by formal verification<sup>[6](/projects/#pluggable-analysis), [1](/projects/#lean4-proof-mining)</sup>, and how they scaffold more aligned, accountable automated systems<sup>[4](/projects/#src), [5](/projects/#ai-glossary)</sup>
